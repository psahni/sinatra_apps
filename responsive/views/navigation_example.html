8 Ways Media SA is a Swiss-based media agency providing 8 main services including branding, web design, IT development, online and offline marketing, entertainment, IT Support and consulting. 8 Ways Media specializes in providing services that focus on creativity as well as the latest designs and technological trends.

Throughout 2013, 8 Ways Media serviced more than 150 clients from across the globe and continues to expand its reach. Our team is made up of more than 35 talented individuals who are motivated and passionate about providing the best possible level of service to clients in an attractive & engaging manner.
